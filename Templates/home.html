Power Ranger Sporting Club

Welcome to the official page of Power Ranger Sporting Club (PRSC)! We are a passionate, high-energy team driven by our love for football, cricket, and a wide range of sports. But PRSC isn’t just about playing games—we’re here to make an impact beyond the field.

We believe in the power of teamwork, dedication, and community spirit. As volunteers for various social causes, we aim to lead with unity, purpose, and heart.

Join us on our journey—let’s play hard, serve others, and grow together!
