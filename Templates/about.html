About Power Ranger Sporting Club (PRSC)

Power Ranger Sporting Club (PRSC) is more than just a sports team. Founded with the vision of fostering passion, teamwork, and community service, PRSC brings together sports enthusiasts who love football, cricket, and many other games. Our goal is to build a strong, supportive community where every member grows both on and off the field.

  • Teamwork: Winning together, supporting each other.
  • Discipline & Dedication: Always giving our best.
  • Community Service: Volunteering and making a positive impact.
  • Inclusivity: Welcoming players and volunteers from all backgrounds.

Whether competing in local tournaments or organizing social initiatives, PRSC is committed to excellence and unity. Join us to be a part of this exciting journey where sports meet service.
